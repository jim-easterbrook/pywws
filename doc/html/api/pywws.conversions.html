pywws.conversions¶

A set of functions to convert pywws native units (Centigrade, mm, m/s, hPa) to other popular units.

Functions

pressure_inhg(hPa) Convert pressure from hectopascals/millibar to inches of mercury
rain_inch(mm) Convert rainfall from millimetres to inches
temp_f(c) Convert temperature from Celsius to Fahrenheit
wind_bft(ms) Convert (wind) wind from metres per second to Beaufort scale
wind_kmph(ms) Convert (wind) wind from metres per second to kilometres per hour
wind_kn(ms) Convert (wind) wind from metres per second to knots
wind_mph(ms) Convert (wind) wind from metres per second to miles per hour
